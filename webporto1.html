About The Photographer

Discover the story behind the lens

Alexandra Moore

With over 15 years of professional photography experience, Alexandra specializes in capturing moments that tell profound visual stories. Her work has been featured in prestigious publications like National Geographic, Vogue, and The New York Times.

Trained at the International Center of Photography in New York, Alexandra brings a unique artistic vision to every project. From commercial assignments to personal fine art photography, she approaches each subject with technical precision and emotional depth.

When not behind the camera, Alexandra leads photography workshops around the world, sharing her knowledge and passion with aspiring photographers.
